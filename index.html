    Data Science


    Data science services include data science consulting, development and support to enable companies to run experiments on their data in search of business insights. CoalDev has been applying data science in its different forms such as fraud and risk detection, healthcare, speech recognition along with statistics and machine learning (including its most recent technique – deep learning) to meet the most deliberate analytics needs of our clients.


    Cloud Solutions


    CoalDev is experienced in working with all major cloud providers — including Amazon Web Services, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud Platform — to help organizations accelerate their transformation and get the most from high-performing cloud environments, whether public, private, or hybrid. We provide custom cloud software development services that enable enterprises and tech innovators to future-proof their infrastructure investments.


    Website Development


    CoalDev provides website development services for the clients around the globe. A website is the central tool for the visibility of a business on the Internet. An effective website will not only strengthen the attraction, but also the acquisition and retention of new customers.


    BlockChain


    Blockchain technology is the latest form of technology with significant security and transparency features. Blockchain has found extensive application in many areas – from finance to hospitality and medicine. The reason for this is the security features of the blockchain. Blockchain adds value and brings benefits to businesses by offering more transparency, increased speed, reduced cost, and enhanced traceability. CoalDev uses blockchain technology to transform financial services, supply chains, government, healthcare, retail, and many other industries.


    ETL


    A typical ETL process collects and refines different types of data, then delivers the data to a data warehouse such as Redshift, Azure, or BigQuery. ETL also makes it possible to migrate data between a variety of sources, destinations, and analysis tools. CoalDev uses ETL to produce data for business intelligence and to execute broader data management strategies.


    Forecasting Solutions


    Forecasting is an efficient and vital data science solution for retail, eCommerce, and many other companies that helps them keep track of products and predict future numbers supported by historical data. This method allows businesses to form rational and important decisions on determining the optimal quantity of products and meeting the longer-term customer demand. CoalDev builds predictive models for different types of business that would use historical data to analyze and forecast future numbers.


    E-Commerece


    Ecommerce or electronic commerce actually means the use of an electronic medium for commercial transactions, but it is commonly used to refer to selling products and services over the internet to consumers or other businesses. CoalDev provides you with Application services, Brokerage services, Data or Transaction Management, Interface and Support Layers, Secure Messaging, Security and Electronic Document Interchange and the basic Communication Services.

CoalDev provides you with custom build digital marketing solutions and software that can help you in social media management, automation, content marketing, and community building.

    Retail & FMCG


    Fast-moving consumer goods are items with high turnover rates, low prices, or short shelf-lives. FMCG sector has been revolutionized with the advancement in technology. CoalDev provides you with automated solutions and smooth operational applications to increase your profit margins and enhance the capacity of business growth.
