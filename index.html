ETL


A typical ETL process collects and refines different types of data, then delivers the data to a data warehouse such as Redshift, Azure, or BigQuery. ETL also makes it possible to migrate data between a variety of sources, destinations, and analysis tools. CoalDev uses ETL to produce data for business intelligence and to execute broader data management strategies.


Forecasting Solutions


Forecasting is an efficient and vital data science solution for retail, eCommerce, and many other companies that helps them keep track of products and predict future numbers supported by historical data. This method allows businesses to form rational and important decisions on determining the optimal quantity of products and meeting the longer-term customer demand. CoalDev builds predictive models for different types of business that would use historical data to analyze and forecast future numbers.
